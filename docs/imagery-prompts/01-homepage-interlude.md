## Locked prompt

> Abstract architectural interior rendered as pure geometry — a cinematic, atmospheric
> still. Long sightlines and structural beams or columns meeting at oblique angles; soft
> architectural depth receding into shadow. The subject is **light**: late-afternoon warm
> amber light raking through the structure, pooling on one plane and falling off into deep
> shadow, with fine dust suspended in the beam. Cinematic shallow depth of field, one plane
> in focus. Empty, designed, utterly still — the feeling of a space at the end of the
> working day.
>
> **Palette — strict, warm only (black · oxblood · ochre · bone):** deepest shadows and
> structure in near-black warm oxblood `#100404`–`#310c0d` (oklch 13–22% hue 22); the
> signature oxblood `#6b1e21` (oklch 36% 0.11 hue 22) present in the mid-darks; the light
> source and its warm pooling in amber/ochre `#a05b11`→`#dc9322`→`#ebbb6e` (oklch 54–82%,
> hue 60–78); lit surfaces and highlights resolving to warm bone `#ece2de`→`#fef9f5`
> (oklch 92–98.5%). **No cool tones whatsoever — no blue, teal, purple, green, grey.**
>
> **Composition:** wide 16:9, asymmetric, one or two dominant geometric forms, long
> oblique structural lines, generous negative space (≈40% of the frame calm, on the left)
> for text overlay. Premium editorial register — high-end architectural photography
> (Tadao Ando, Peter Zumthor) crossed with an abstract cinematic still.
>
> **Mood:** quiet, considered, premium, restrained. Light pooling, dust in air, complete
> stillness. Not dramatic, not moody-for-its-own-sake.

**Negative / exclude:** people, human figures, hands, faces, silhouettes; workshops,
workbenches, studios, tools, pottery, kilns; any manufacturing or manual labour; software
UI, screens, code, dashboards, circuitry; blue, teal, cyan, purple, magenta, green; cool
greys; purple-to-blue gradients, sparkles, neon, lens flare, fake bokeh, hyperreal CGI
sheen; text, logos, watermarks.
